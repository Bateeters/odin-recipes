Cauliflower Mac & Cheese

Description

A carbo-loaded vegetarian mac and cheese recipe. This is a perfect way to sneak some veggies onto a picky eater's plate. And we don't even miss the mac!

Ingredients

  • 2 heads of cauliflower (~5 lbs), cored and broken into florets
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • 2 cups of cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil over high heat. While you're waiting on that to boil, rinse and chop the cauliflower into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Add the cauliflower and cook until just tender, about 5 minutes. Drain and return it to the pot over low heat.
  3. Add both the sour cream and cheddar, stir it in until cheddar has melted.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste (other seasonings are optional).
  5. Remove the pot from heat and plate servings. Sprinkle with parsley and serve with soup or salad.

Notes:

  • Greek yogurt can be substituted for the sour cream if desired.
  • Try to use block cheese instead of shredded as it has a powder coating to prevent it from melting.
  • Cheddar can be substituted for any kind of cheese you prefer.
